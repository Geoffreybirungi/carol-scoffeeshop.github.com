About Our Company

Welcome to Carol Coffee Shop!

Established in 2019,located in Fort Portal Tourism City, Uganda.

Carol Coffee Shop has been proudly serving the finest coffee drinks to our valued customers. We are passionate about providing a warm and inviting atmosphere where coffee lovers can indulge in their favorite beverages and create lasting memories. At Carol Coffee Shop, we believe that coffee is more than just a beverage; it's an experience. That's why we strive to deliver excellence in every cup we serve. Our dedicated team of skilled baristas is trained to craft each drink with precision and care, ensuring that every sip is a moment of pure delight.

Whether you prefer a smooth and creamy latte, a robust and invigorating espresso, or a refreshing iced coffee, we have the perfect blend for you.
